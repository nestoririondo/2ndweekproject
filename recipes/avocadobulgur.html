Avocado Bulgur Salad

20 min

Easy

This refreshing Avocado Bulgur Salad combines the creamy goodness of avocados with the nutty flavor of bulgur, creating a light and satisfying dish perfect for any occasion.

1 cup Bulgur
2 Ripe avocados, diced
1 cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cup Red onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup Fresh cilantro, chopped
1/4 cup Feta cheese, crumbled
3 tbsp Extra-virgin olive oil
2 tbsp Lime juice
Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

Step 1: Cook the bulgur according to package instructions. Once cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool to room temperature.

Step 2: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled bulgur, diced avocados, halved cherry tomatoes, finely chopped red onion, chopped fresh cilantro, and crumbled feta cheese.

Step 3: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4: Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients and gently toss to coat everything evenly.

Step 5: Taste the salad and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Refrigerate for at least 10 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
