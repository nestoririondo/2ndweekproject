Buttermilk Banana Cake

45 min

Easy

Indulge your taste buds with the moist and flavorful Buttermilk Banana Cake. This easy-to-make cake combines the richness of buttermilk with the sweetness of ripe bananas for a delightful treat.

2 cups All-purpose flour
1 teaspoon Baking powder
1/2 teaspoon Baking soda
1/2 teaspoon Salt
1/2 cup Unsalted butter, softened
1 cup Granulated sugar
2 Large eggs
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1 cup Buttermilk
3 Ripe bananas, mashed
Cream cheese frosting
Sliced bananas for garnish

Preparation

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a cake pan.

Step 2: In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

Step 3: In another bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.

Step 4: Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients alternately with the buttermilk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Fold in the mashed bananas.

Step 5: Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Step 6: Allow the cake to cool completely. Frost with cream cheese frosting and garnish with sliced bananas. Slice and enjoy your Buttermilk Banana Cake!
