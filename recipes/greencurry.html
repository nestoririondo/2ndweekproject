Green Curry Lentil Soup

40 min

Intermediate

Experience a burst of Thai flavors with this Green Curry Lentil Soup. Packed with lentils, vegetables, and aromatic green curry paste, it's a comforting and spicy soup that will warm your soul.

1 cup Green lentils, rinsed
1 Onion, finely chopped
2 Carrots, diced
2 cloves Garlic, minced
1 can (14 oz) Coconut milk
2 tbsp Green curry paste
4 cups Vegetable broth
1 Lime, juiced
Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

Step 1: In a large pot, saut√© the chopped onion, diced carrots, and minced garlic until softened.

Step 2: Add the green lentils, coconut milk, and green curry paste to the pot. Stir well to combine.

Step 3: Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 25-30 minutes or until the lentils are tender.

Step 4: Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. Squeeze in the juice of one lime and stir to incorporate the flavors.

Step 5: Serve the Green Curry Lentil Soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and additional lime wedges if desired.
