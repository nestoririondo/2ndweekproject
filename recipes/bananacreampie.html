Banana Cream Pie

4 hours (including chilling time)

Intermediate

Indulge in the creamy and dreamy goodness of Banana Cream Pie. This classic dessert features a buttery pie crust, velvety banana custard, fresh banana slices, and a cloud-like whipped cream topping.

1 1/2 cups Graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup Unsalted butter, melted
1 cup Granulated sugar
1/4 cup Cornstarch
1/4 teaspoon Salt
2 1/2 cups Whole milk
4 Egg yolks, lightly beaten
2 tablespoons Unsalted butter
2 teaspoons Vanilla extract
4 Bananas, sliced
1 1/2 cups Heavy cream
2 tablespoons Confectioners' sugar

Preparation

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture into a pie dish to form the crust. Bake for 10 minutes, then let it cool.

Step 2: In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Gradually whisk in the milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens.

Step 3: In a separate bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks. Gradually whisk in about 1 cup of the hot milk mixture. Pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan and cook, stirring, until thickened. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla extract.

Step 4: Layer sliced bananas over the cooled crust. Pour the custard over the bananas, spreading it evenly. Chill the pie in the refrigerator for at least 3 hours or until set.

Step 5: In a chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream and confectioners' sugar until stiff peaks form. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie.

Step 6: Garnish with additional banana slices if desired. Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy your delightful Banana Cream Pie!
